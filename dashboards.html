Bike Sales Dashboard

A comprehensive sales analysis dashboard for a bike retail business tracking 1,465 orders with a total due of $33.93M. The report delivers a full view of business performance across four key visuals: monthly order trends revealing seasonal peaks, an order status breakdown (Approved 27% · In-Process 27% · Shipped 26% · Cancelled 9%), product category performance with Bikes leading at 1,200+ orders, and a territory revenue treemap spanning Canada, Northwest USA, UK, France, and Australia.

AdventureWorks 2022

Sales Performance — AdventureWorks

A multi-page report powered by the AdventureWorks 2022 SQL Server database, covering 31K orders, $123.22M total due, and 275K items shipped.

Page 1 — Overview: High-level KPIs with a daily order timeline (Ship Date vs Due Date), category breakdown (Accessories 20K · Bikes 18K · Clothing 10K), territory performance chart, and a 2011–2014 date slicer with region and shipping filters.

Page 2 — Territory Map: Global bubble map showing order volume by territory, regional group pie chart (North America 51% · Europe 27% · Pacific 22%), and shipping method comparison between XRQ Truck Ground and Cargo Transport 5.

Page 3 — Sales Persons: Top 10 salesperson ranking by total revenue — Linda Mitchell leads at $0.38bn — with full-name and shipping-method slicers for deep-dive analysis.

AdventureWorks 2022

Advanced Analytics — AdventureWorks

An advanced single-page analytics report built on AdventureWorks 2022, surfacing 31K orders, 275K total quantity, and $123.22M total due across 6 headline KPI cards. The report features a combined daily timeline that overlays Ship Date, Due Date, and Order Count — giving a clear picture of fulfillment efficiency and bottlenecks. Additional visuals cover territory revenue performance, order status (Shipped dominates at 31K), product category split (Accessories · Bikes · Clothing · Components), and a detailed shipping method breakdown showing XRQ Truck Ground at 27.7K vs Cargo Transport 5 at 3.8K.
